# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Women who care about their body, mind, routines, and self-development — students, creatives, founders, mothers, wellness-minded, often several at once. She wants to feel organized, in control, healthy, and emotionally balanced *without* burning out. She wants structure but also permission to slow down. Context: she opens this in the morning with coffee, mid-afternoon when energy dips, and at night while winding down — not in a rush, looking for a calm intelligent companion, not another nagging productivity app.

## Product Purpose
Selenia is an **adaptive daily life-planning system for women** built around natural energy and cycle patterns rather than rigid, identical-every-day productivity. It adapts the day to her body, energy, mood, cycle, and real-life situations through cycle tracking, AI daily planning, morning/night check-ins, mood tracking, supportive notifications, and an AI assistant. The landing page's job: make a visitor *feel* the calm intelligence of the product and believe "this was made for how I actually live," then join the waitlist. Success = the page reads as a considered, premium personal system — not a tracker, not a girlboss app.

## Brand Personality
Lunar · Calm · Intelligent. Soft but grounded; supportive, never fake-positive; realistic over motivational-poster. Voice speaks *to* her like a perceptive friend who happens to be brilliant — short, warm, exact sentences. The moon/cycle is the organizing metaphor (Selene → Selenia): life as cyclical, not linear.

## Anti-references
NEVER: "girlboss" / hustle energy; childish or cutesy (no bubbly pastels, no emoji confetti); robotic or clinical/medical; corporate SaaS (gradient-metric hero, identical feature-card grid); fake-spiritual / woo; guilt-driven or judgmental; overwhelming. Also avoid the 2026 AI tells: cream/beige body background, display-serif-italic + mono-label editorial template, tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows over every section, pulsing/glowing decorative motion.

## Design Principles
1. **Cyclical, not linear.** Every structural and motion decision should echo the cycle — curves, orbits, waves, returns — not rigid straight lines and grids.
2. **Calm is the feature.** Restraint, generous space, slow ambient motion. The page should lower the visitor's heart rate, not raise it.
3. **Show the intelligence, don't claim it.** Demonstrate adaptivity with real figures (energy curve, day-arc, phase ring, sample check-in) instead of buzzwords.
4. **Supportive, realistic register.** Copy gives permission to slow down; it never shouts, never fake-cheerleads.
5. **One hand, one world.** Coherent teal-lunar art direction throughout; figures are bespoke, never stock-icon filler.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1, verified against teal and light surfaces. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` path — all ambient/looping motion stops and resolves to a calm static state. No vestibular triggers (no large zoom/parallax/spin). Content fully visible without JS; motion is enhancement only. Keyboard-focusable interactive elements with visible focus rings.
